Momo Recipe

Description

Momo is the best food in the world. It is eaten by most of the Nepali. It is one of the most common food available in Nepal.
Momo is used in many festivals as a common tasty food.

Ingredients

  • Keema
  • Onion
  • Masala
  • Flour
  1. Mix the keema with onion and masala
  2. Rest the keema for about 20-30 minutes
  3. Put the mixed keema inside the flour
  4. Steam it for about an hour
  5. Eat it!
